## Migration Guide

If you encounter issues after upgrading, follow these steps to migrate smoothly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Backup Your Data" icon="database">

    Export all projects via the dashboard.

    ```
    curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/export \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -d '{"projectId": "proj_123"}'
    ```

  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Tokens" icon="key">

    Regenerate JWT tokens in your settings.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Test MDX Pages" icon="file-text">

    Validate frontmatter and escape special characters like `{` and `<`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Changes" icon="upload">

    Push updates to your production environment.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="alert">

**Breaking Change in v1.0.0:** Session-based auth is deprecated. Migrate to JWT tokens immediately to avoid disruptions. See the [migration guide](#migration-guide) above.

</Callout>
