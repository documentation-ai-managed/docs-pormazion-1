## Getting Started

Creating your first project is simple:

1. Log in at `https://app.pormazion.com`
2. Click "New Project" on your dashboard
3. Enter a project name and description
4. Choose a template or start blank
5. Click "Create"

Your documentation space is now ready! Projects can be edited, organized, and shared.
