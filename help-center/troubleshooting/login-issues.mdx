---
title: Can't Log In to PORMAZION
description: Steps to resolve login problems.
---

## Symptoms
- Login button not working
- Invalid credentials message

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
<Step title="Check credentials">
  Ensure your email and password are correct. Reset password if needed.
</Step>
<Step title="Clear cache">
  Clear browser cache and cookies and try again.
</Step>
<Step title="Try another browser">
  Use Chrome or Firefox to rule out browser issues.
</Step>
<Step title="Still stuck?">
  <Callout kind="info">Contact support at support@pormazion.com.</Callout>
</Step>
</Steps>
