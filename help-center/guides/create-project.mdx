## Quick Start Guide

<Steps>
<Step title="Access PORMAZION">
  Go to `https://app.pormazion.com` and log in.
</Step>
<Step title="Start new project">
  Click "+ New Project" at the top of your dashboard.
</Step>
<Step title="Fill in details">
  Enter your project name, description, and select a template as needed.
</Step>
<Step title="Finish">
  Click "Create". Your documentation project is now ready to use.
</Step>
</Steps>
